What Crowdless does

Crowdless helps you avoid overcrowded areas. It estimates how busy popular places will be and suggests quieter, high‑quality alternatives nearby. The goal: shorter lines, calmer visits, and a fairer spread of visitors across a city or region.

Under the hood, Crowdless learns from European Earth‑observation data (Copernicus Sentinel satellites) and location signals powered by Galileo. You don’t need to understand the tech — you just open a simple map, see when and where it’s busy, and pick the right time or another great place to go.

Features & How it works

Crowd forecasts

Smart alternatives

Live map & alerts

Check a clean map view and get notifications that nudge you at the right moment.

Built on EU space tech
