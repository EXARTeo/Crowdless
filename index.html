The Core Problem

Recommending alternatives at scale
creates new crowds.

Classic systems send everyone to the same “quiet” alternative — making it the next hotspot. Crowdless is built to be aware of its own impact.

Try the City Simulator →

How It Works

A reinforcement learning loop.

Crowdless learns from the outcomes of its own recommendations, closing the loop so predictions get smarter over time.

Who Benefits

Built for people.
Designed for cities.

For Travelers
  • No more second-crowd trap

    Get recommendations that are genuinely quiet — not somewhere we already sent hundreds of users.

  • Friction-aware routing

    Minimizes extra walking distance. Your time is factored into every recommendation.

  • Accurate arrivals

    Venues are at the occupancy we predicted — not over-capacity from our own recommendations.

For Cities & Operators
  • Reduced peak concentration

    Visitor flow is spread across venues and time windows, reducing hotspots.

  • Self-correcting system

    The RL policy learns when it over-recommends and backs off automatically.

  • Equitable traffic distribution

    Visitor load is shared fairly across local businesses — not funneled to the same few.
